Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program for 3-5 year olds focuses on social-emotional development, literacy, and numeracy skills, laying the foundation for lifelong learning.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, students in grades K-5 engage in project-based learning, developing critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs for grades 6-8 focus on personalized learning, academic rigor, and social-emotional growth, preparing students for high school and beyond.
